## Brand Overview

- **What it is:** VeeFriends is Gary Vaynerchuk's ("Gary Vee") character brand, launched
  **May 2021** as a hand-drawn NFT collection and since expanded into a full IP company
  spanning comics, trading cards, toys, apparel, animation, and events (VeeCon).
- **Core philosophy:** Every character embodies a **positive human trait or skill** Gary
  believes leads to happiness and success — empathy, patience, accountability, ambition,
  gratitude, etc. The brand's mission: "create a better world through characters that
  encourage us to be our best selves."
- **Naming convention:** Alliterative *Adjective + Animal* names — Empathy Elephant,
  Patient Panda, Brave Bison, Legendary Lemur. A handful are non-animals (Willful Wizard,
  Rare Robot, Notorious Ninja, Kind Warrior, Motivated Monster).
- **Series 1 (May 11, 2021):** 10,255 NFTs covering **268 characters**, every one
  personally hand-drawn by Gary. Doubled as tickets to VeeCon 2022–2024. The "grail"
  collectible of the brand.
- **Series 2 (2022):** 55,555 tokens; **251 characters** (236 returning + 15 new) rendered
  as fully illustrated, colorful, animated versions across 74 scenes and 6 poses. Character
  counts vary by source (268 / 283 / 251) depending on series and additions.
- **Physical expansion:**
  - **Topps Chrome VeeFriends** (long-term Topps deal): 2025 debut set — 100-card base,
    refractor parallels, 1/1 Superfractors, inserts like **Game On** (retro 8-bit pixel
    style), **ERUPT!**, **Iconics** (stained-glass style), Manga Speckle. The **2026 set
    (June 10)** grows to **200 characters** with 149 "First Chrome" appearances, plus a
    Sapphire Edition.
  - **Compete & Collect TCG:** every character card has **Aura + Skill + Stamina = Score**
    with rarity multipliers; head-to-head card-flip battles.
  - Also: Funko Pops, Squishmallows, pins, Super Stickers, Reebok/Crocs collabs, comics,
    a Moonbug-produced kids' cartoon, and "Burn Island" (trade digital assets for physical
    rewards via "Eruptions").

## Design Aesthetic

- **Original look:** Gary's own loose, imperfect **hand-drawn doodle style** — wobbly
  linework, simple shapes, big expressive eyes, handwritten character names. Charmingly
  amateur on purpose; the sketch quality is the brand signature.
- **Series 2 look:** professional, **vibrant flat-color cartoon illustration** of the same
  characters — bold outlines, saturated palettes, dynamic poses, scenic backgrounds
  (jungle, castle, etc.).
- **Licensed alt-styles** the brand itself embraces (useful precedent for a game):
  **Game On 8-bit pixel art** (Topps insert — Legendary Lemur is in it), manga style,
  stained-glass "Iconics," fractured WPAP geometric style (artist Gilang Bogy), and
  J. Scott Campbell comic covers.
- **Tone:** optimistic, kind, motivational, playful humor; self-improvement themes are
  always front and center. Even "negative" characters exist to teach a lesson.
- **Game takeaway:** a doodle/hand-drawn aesthetic or an 8-bit pixel style are both
  authentically on-brand; pair with bright flat colors and trait-based messaging.

## Legendary Lemur (Player Character)

- **Trait meaning:** Legacy-building through bold action — "**Legends are made with every
  bold leap.**" Ambition and the pursuit of greatness, expressed through daily service:
  he wakes each morning asking *"How can I be of service today?"* True legends lift others
  while chasing their own aspirations.
- **Motto (verified):** "**LEAPING INTO LEGEND, ONE BRANCH AT A TIME**" — used on his
  card/character materials. Perfect literal fit for a platformer.
- **Personality/bio:** Known for bold acts of kindness and compassion; builds legacy
  through deliberate, courageous leaps rather than waiting for recognition. Frequently
  adventures with **Arbitraging Admiral**.
- **Fun fact (canon):** Addicted to **treasure hunts and solving cryptic puzzles** — a
  ready-made justification for collectibles, hidden areas, and puzzle rooms.
- **Competitive advantage (canon):** "Takes great leaps with actions aimed at building a
  memorable legacy."
- **Appearance:** A lemur with **large, expressive eyes** radiating curiosity and
  determination; card art poses convey **movement, energy, and leaping**. Series 1/2 NFT
  metadata: Category *Mammal*, Property/scene *Jungle*, Rarity *Rare*. Exists in Gary's
  doodle style, Series 2 full-color style, manga style, and the pixelated **Game On**
  style. (Exact palette should be checked against official art; classic ring-tailed
  gray/white body with black-ringed tail is the expected read — verify before final art.)
- **Compete & Collect stats (canon):** **Score 70 — Aura 23, Skill 24, Stamina 23**
  (a strong, well-balanced character; Patient Panda's 74 is among the highest seen).
- **Topps Chrome:** Base card **#52** in 2025 Topps Chrome VeeFriends; also featured in
  the **Game On 8-bit insert** with Blue /99, Purple /50, Green /25, Yellow /10, Teal /5,
  and Superfractor /1 parallels. Refractor/Shimmer parallels exist of the base card.

## Lore & Settings

- **VeeWorld:** the overarching setting of the VeeFriends comics — "wondrous lands"
  the characters adventure through (Comic #3 *World Tour*).
- **Frowning Forest (verified):** featured in **Comic Book #4, "Hunt for Harpik"** —
  Willful Wizard hires three adventurers to find the *Thoughtful Three-Horned Harpik* in
  the Frowning Forest (moral: verify what you're told before believing it). Also appears
  in a 2025 daily comic, "Willful Wizard Wanders Through Frowning Forest." Great spooky
  mid-game biome.
- **Honey Empire Castle:** from *The Origin of Fearless Fairy* (Comic #2) — where fairies
  bloom; Patient Panda travels there. Fairy-kingdom / castle level material.
- **Gratitude Garden:** built by Patient Panda, Hard-Working Wombat, and Persistent
  Penguin — a peaceful hub-world candidate.
- **Burn Island:** the brand's real-world burn-to-redeem platform with "Eruptions" —
  thematically a volcano/lava level candidate.
- **The Fear Carrot:** the corrupting object from *The Origin of Fearless Fairy*; seizing
  it (out of gluttony) is what turned Bad Intentions bad. Ideal MacGuffin for a boss arc.
- **Comic storylines** (for tone reference): *The Battle for Balance* (#1, helping a
  grieving teen), *Origin of Fearless Fairy* (#2, good vs. evil sibling fairies),
  *World Tour* (#3), *Hunt for Harpik* (#4), plus solo books (Motivated Monster, Versatile
  Viking, Notorious Ninja, Gary Bee, Kind Warrior). Stories always carry an emotional
  life-lesson.

**Negative-trait character verification:** VeeFriends is overwhelmingly positive; the
confirmed negative/cautionary characters are **Cynical Cat**, **Bad Intentions**, and
**Suffocate Hate** (an anti-hate concept character). **"Gossip Gorilla," "Gossiping
Goat," and "Impatient Iguana" do NOT exist** (the real ones are Gift Goat and Intuitive
Iguana). "Truculent T-Rex" (Comic #2) is the only other edgy-named character found.
Likewise "Adventurous Ant" and "Determined Dodo" don't exist — the real characters are
**Adventurous Astronaut** and **Dope Dodo**; it's **Courageous Cockatoo** (though a
Confident Cobra also exists) and **Empathy Elephant** (not "Empathetic").
